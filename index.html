Data Analysis  ·  Cost Optimization  ·  EllisDon

Zero Usage Mobile Line Optimization

Corporate Telecom Spend Analysis — 2025

200+Lines Analysed
$20K+Annual Savings
8moDuration
Excel CimpL Telus Portal Data Validation Cost Analysis

In early 2025, a wave of company-wide terminations at EllisDon created an unexpected data problem: hundreds of corporate mobile lines were sitting idle, quietly accumulating costs with zero usage.

Data Source: Usage reports were pulled from CimpL, a telecom management platform connected to our Telus account, delivered as Excel exports flagging each line as Active or Vacation.

Validation & Cleansing: Every record was cross-referenced against the live Telus portal to verify status accuracy, resolve discrepancies, and confirm data completeness — ensuring no active lines were incorrectly flagged.

Analysis & Decision: Initial scope targeted lines under $400 remaining balance. Deeper analysis revealed far broader cost exposure. I recommended cancelling all zero-usage lines regardless of balance — absorbing short-term costs for long-term savings.

Audit Trail: A structured cancellation log tracked line numbers, contract balances, statuses, and dates across 8 months of activity.

Outcome: 200+ lines cancelled. $20,000+ in projected annual savings delivered.

Data without action is just noise. The value was in the decision-making it enabled — not just the analysis itself.
