Zero Usage Mobile Line Optimization

Corporate Telecom Spend Analysis — 2025

Excel Data Validation Cost Analysis Dataset Cross-Referencing ServiceNow

This project involved end-to-end analysis of corporate mobile line data to identify cost inefficiencies and drive a structured cancellation initiative across the organization's telecom infrastructure.

Data Source & Ingestion: Telecom usage data was sourced from a third-party platform and delivered as Excel exports, with each record flagging line status as Active or Vacation. The dataset covered all corporate mobile lines assigned to managed office locations.

Validation & Cleansing: Each record was cross-referenced against the live carrier portal to verify status accuracy, resolve discrepancies, and confirm data completeness. This step was critical to ensuring no active lines were incorrectly flagged for cancellation.

Analysis & Decision Framework: Initial scope targeted lines with under $400 remaining contract balance. Upon deeper analysis, the volume of dormant lines indicated a broader cost exposure. A revised recommendation was made to cancel all zero-usage lines regardless of balance — absorbing short-term contract costs in exchange for long-term operational savings.

Tracking & Audit Trail: A structured cancellation log was maintained throughout, documenting line numbers, contract balances, statuses, and cancellation dates — providing full traceability across 8 months of activity.

Outcome: 200+ lines cancelled. Projected annual savings of $20,000+ delivered to the organization.

This project reinforced something I believe in: data without action is just noise. The value was in the decision-making it enabled — not just the analysis itself.
